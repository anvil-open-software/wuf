Toast service

We have 4 types of toasts:

Click on the links to get a toast / snackbar:

info toast

success toast

warning toast

error toast

Under the hood toasts service is using material snackbar.

Some examples on how to use it.

When you want to tell the user an action was been successfull, failed, had some warnings. You can also use this when you want to inform the user with a simple message.

Add WufToastsService to your constructor of your component and call the methods, info, success, error, warning with a message to show the toasts.
