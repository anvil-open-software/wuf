Utilities

The @anviltech/wuf-ang-library package provides a number of different utilities for use throughout your application.

Capitalize Pipe

This utility will capitalize the first character of any string.

Examples:

Capitalize {{string_1}} to {{string_1 | kgSentenceCase}}

Capitalize {{string_2}} to {{string_2 | kgSentenceCase}}

Capitalize {{string_3}} to {{string_3 | kgSentenceCase}}

Capitalize {{string_4}} to {{string_4 | kgSentenceCase}}

Capitalize {{string_5}} to {{string_5 | kgSentenceCase}}
